Bytecode Programming Language

Welcome to Bytecode — the ultra-hard, stack-based programming language that compiles to bytecode. Write your programs in .bc files and run them with the Bytecode VM.

Download Bytecode VM

Windows (.exe) Linux

Quick Start Example

Save this as hello.bc and run it with your VM:

10 48 19 10 45 19 10 4C 19 10 4C 19 10 4F 19 10 20 19 
10 57 19 10 4F 19 10 52 19 10 4C 19 10 44 19 10 0A 19
FF

Then run:

./bytecode_vm hello.bc      # Linux
bytecode_vm.exe hello.bc    # Windows

Enjoy creating insanely hard programs in Bytecode 😈
